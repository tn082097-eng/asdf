Biography & Career

MacKenzie Scott Tuttle is an American novelist and philanthropist. She is best known for her unprecedented approach to charitable giving, having donated over $26.3 billion to more than 1,600 organizations since 2019 — one of the largest philanthropic efforts by any individual in history.

"Teams with experience on the front lines of challenges will know best how to put the money to good use."
